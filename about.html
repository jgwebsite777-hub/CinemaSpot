About CinemaSpot


Data Retrival:

First we pull information from all these websites. Such as IMDb; for its large Database of ratings basd on views of the movies, The Movie Ranking Database; for the fan perspective, Rotten Tomatoes; for the quick ratings of the different factors(plot, diologue, Rewatchablility and more) that go into great movies, and some of our websites prefrerence.

Standardization

Next we bring this information together about the movies and we change there individual ratings to out of 100% to get them on an even feild.

"Super Score" Calculation

Finally we add up all these values and get the Super score displayed with every Movie on this site. (Ratigns can be altered if needed)
