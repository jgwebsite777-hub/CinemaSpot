How We Rate
Our ratings are based on a formula of multiple factors.
Rewatchablility
Rewatchability is the main denomenator of any movie so it takes up 35% of the rating.
Acting
We base the acting on a scale of 20%.
Story line
This includes things like plot, build up and resolution all into one. This is 30% of the rating.
Tone
This includes what the watcher gets from the film and how much the movie effects you. This is 25% of the rating.
All of these factors are added up to create the rating of every movie on this website.
