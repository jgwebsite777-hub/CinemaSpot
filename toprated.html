2.Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith 100%

Description

Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith (Directed by George Lucas) is widely hailed as the darkest and most climactic chapter of the prequel trilogy, following the tragic fall of Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker (Hayden Christensen) as he is manipulated by the sinister Chancellor Palpatine (Ian McDiarmid) into betraying the Jedi Order to save his wife, Padmé Amidala (Natalie Portman), featuring the devastating execution of Order 66, the rise of the Galactic Empire, and a legendary, fiery duel on the volcanic planet Mustafar against his mentor, Obi-Wan Kenobi (Ewan McGregor).

  • Director: George Lucas
  • Story/Screenplay/Executive Producer: George Lucas
  • Producer: Rick McCallum
  • Cast: Hayden Christensen (Anakin Skywalker), Ewan McGregor (Obi-Wan Kenobi), Natalie Portman (Padmé Amidala), Ian McDiarmid (Chancellor Palpatine), and Samuel L. Jackson (Mace Windu).
  • Key Villain: Darth Sidious (portrayed by Ian McDiarmid) and General Grievous (voiced by Matthew Wood).
  • Key New Character: Commander Cody (portrayed by Temuera Morrison) and Tion Medon (portrayed by Bruce Spence).
  • Academy Awards (2006): Nominated for Best Achievement in Makeup (Dave Elsey and Nikki Gooley).
  • Saturn Awards (2006): Won Best Science Fiction Film and Best Music (John Williams). Nominated for 8 others, including Best Director (George Lucas), Writing, and Actor (Hayden Christensen).
  • Grammy Awards (2006): Nominated for Best Score Soundtrack Album for Motion Picture, Television or Other Visual Media (John Williams).
  • MTV Movie Awards (2006): Won Best Villain (Hayden Christensen). Nominated for Best Fight (Anakin Skywalker vs. Obi-Wan Kenobi) and Best Hero (Ewan McGregor).
  • People's Choice Awards (2006): Won Favorite Movie and Favorite Movie Drama.
  • Empire Awards (2006): Won Scene of the Year (The Birth of Vader). Nominated for Best Sci-Fi/Fantasy.



3. Rogue One 100%

Description

Rogue One: A Star Wars Story (Directed by Gareth Edwards) is widely hailed as a gritty and visceral masterpiece for its grounded, high-stakes portrayal of the rebellion's desperate struggle, following the reluctant hero Jyn Erso (Felicity Jones) and a band of unlikely outcasts as they embark on a daring mission to steal the plans for the Death Star, featuring breathtaking planetary battles, the philosophical tension of war, and an unforgettable, climactic sacrifice that leads directly into the events of the original 1977 film.

  • Director: Gareth Edwards
  • Story/Executive Producer: John Knoll and Gary Whitta (Story) / John Knoll (Executive Producer)
  • Screenplay: Chris Weitz and Tony Gilroy
  • Producer: Kathleen Kennedy, Allison Shearmur, and Simon Emanuel
  • Cast: Felicity Jones (Jyn Erso), Diego Luna (Cassian Andor), Alan Tudyk (K-2SO), Donnie Yen (Chirrut Îmwe), and Riz Ahmed (Bodhi Rook).
  • Key Villain: Director Orson Krennic (portrayed by Ben Mendelsohn) and Grand Moff Tarkin (portrayed by Guy Henry, with the likeness of Peter Cushing).
  • Key New Character: K-2SO (voiced/motion-capture by Alan Tudyk) and Chirrut Îmwe (portrayed by Donnie Yen).
  • Academy Awards (2017): Nominated for 2 Oscars: Best Visual Effects and Best Sound Mixing.
  • Saturn Awards (2017): Won 3 awards: Best Science Fiction Film, Best Director (Gareth Edwards), and Best Special Effects. It led the year with 11 total nominations, including Best Actress (Felicity Jones) and Best Supporting Actor (Diego Luna).
  • Empire Awards (2017): Won 3 major prizes: Best Film, Best Actress (Felicity Jones), and Best Director (Gareth Edwards).
  • BAFTA Awards (2017): Nominated for 2 awards: Best Special Visual Effects and Best Make Up & Hair.
  • MTV Movie & TV Awards (2017): Nominated for 3 awards, including Movie of the Year, Best Hero (Felicity Jones), and Next Generation (Riz Ahmed).
  • Visual Effects Society Awards (2017): Led the feature film field with 7 nominations, including Outstanding Visual Effects in a Photoreal Feature.

9. Star Wars: The Force Awakens 98%

Description

It follows scavenger Rey, rogue stormtrooper Finn, and pilot Poe Dameron as they join the Resistance to find Luke Skywalker and fight Kylo Ren and the First Order. The film, which blended practical effects with new characters and returning legacy heroes, was a massive critical and box office success.
